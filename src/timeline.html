Act 1, Scene 1

We start off with a conversation between Duncan and the Captain. The Captain gives us some information about Macbeth. Duncan states that 'Dismayed not this our captains, Macbeth and Banquo?' From that statement alone we now know that Macbeth must be somewhat brave and heroic if they are a captain. The following statement from the Captain, 'So they doubly redoubled strokes upon the foe.' supports these claims.
